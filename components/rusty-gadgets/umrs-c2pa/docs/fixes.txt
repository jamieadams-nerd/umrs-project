  That tells the full story: OpenAI issued a cert to Truepic's signing tool (integrated into Sora), which was invoked
  by ChatGPT to sign the image. The footnote explains why trust wasn't evaluated. Clean.



Truepic, by the way — they're a content authenticity company. OpenAI contracted with them for C2PA signing
  infrastructure. That's why the cert CN says "Truepic Lens CLI in Sora" — it's Truepic's signing tool running inside
  OpenAI's Sora image generation pipeline. The cert was issued by OpenAI (they're the CA), but Truepic's tool does the
  actual signing operation.
