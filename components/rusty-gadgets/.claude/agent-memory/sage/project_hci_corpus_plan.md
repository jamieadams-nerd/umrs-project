**Sage-specific relevance:** Genre theory (Miller/Bazerman) is directly applicable to
outreach work. Understanding that a blog post, a conference abstract, a white paper, and
a deployment guide are different genres with different social functions — not just different
formats — improves every content decision. The Cognitive Dimensions framework applies to
evaluating whether outreach copy communicates the right things about UMRS's complexity.

**Why this matters for outreach:** The IA/KO material gives Sage better vocabulary for
describing what UMRS does at the classification/organization level — the CUI taxonomy,
the posture signal catalog, the trust tier structure. These are information architecture
decisions with KO theoretical backing. Being able to name that is useful in academic and
Five Eyes contexts.
