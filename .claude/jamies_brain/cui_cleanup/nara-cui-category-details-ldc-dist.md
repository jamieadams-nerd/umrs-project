# NARA CUI Category Detail Scrape — LDC and Distribution Statement Requirements

Source: Individual category detail pages at https://www.archives.gov/cui/registry/category-detail/

## Summary

Of 124 categories scraped, the vast majority have **no LDC or Distribution Statement requirements**.

### Categories WITH Distribution Statement Requirements

| Category | Marking | Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| Controlled Technical Information | CTI | Distribution Statements B through F, per DoD Instruction 5230.24 |

### Categories WITH Limited Dissemination Controls

| Category | Marking | LDC |
|---|---|---|
| Legal Privilege | PRIVILEGE | Attorney-Client (AC) and Attorney Work Product (AWP) — these LDCs may ONLY be used with this category |

### Categories with Special Dissemination Notes (not formal LDC/DST)

| Category | Marking | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Bank Secrecy | FSEC | Must follow FinCEN guidelines; SARs confidential per 31 USC 5318(g)(2); exempt from FOIA |
| Internal Data | ID | "Not intended to be disseminated beyond CIA channels" |
| International Agreement Information | INTL | Control limited to SecDef, SecDHS (Coast Guard), SecEnergy (national security programs) |
| US Census | CENS | Restricted to individuals with "special sworn status" for "statistical purposes only" |
| Tax Convention | CONV | Retains confidential status even when residing with agencies other than IRS |
| NATO Restricted | — | Mark per USSAN Instruction 1-07 and NATO Security Directives |
| NATO Unclassified | — | Mark per USSAN Instruction 1-07; "may only be used for official NATO purposes" |
