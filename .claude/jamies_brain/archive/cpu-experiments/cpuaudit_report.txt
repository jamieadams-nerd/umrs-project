You said:

> I thought you had to do some kind of elfdump on the binaries on the rhel10 system.

Yes. You are 100% correct.

That step is still important, and it becomes **more important now**, not less.

Here’s why:

OpenSSL *says* it was built with:


But UMRS wants **evidence**, not assertions.

So on RHEL 10 (and later here too), we should still capture:

• PIE
• NX
• stack protections
• ABI features
