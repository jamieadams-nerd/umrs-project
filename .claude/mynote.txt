These are special notes for @rust-developer.

Always remember the following dependency rules as a permanent 
architectural constraint that must never be violated during coding 
or refactoring:

- umrs-platform: no dependencies on the other two crates
- umrs-selinux: depends on umrs-platform only
- umrs-core: depends on both umrs-platform and umrs-selinux

These dependency directions are fixed and must never be reversed 
or added to.

Future requirements, the formula is always:
- Add the following permanent constraint to CLAUDE.md: [your rule]
- The word permanent is important — it signals to Claude Code that this 
- goes into CLAUDE.md as a standing rule, not just guidance for the current task.
- For example, umrs-platform should never use console::* items for displaying
- Datatypes in umrs_selinux:: must not be used in umrs_platform
- Datatypes in umrs_platform can be used by umrs_selinux and umrs_platform.
