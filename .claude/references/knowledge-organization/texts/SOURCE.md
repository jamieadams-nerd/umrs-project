## Documents

### Svenonius — The Intellectual Foundation of Information Organization, Chapter 5
- **File:** `svenonius-intellectual-foundation-ch5.pdf`
- **Source:** https://sites.evergreen.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/226/2016/08/svenonius-chap5.pdf
- **Author:** Elaine Svenonius
- **Full book:** MIT Press, 2000
- **Description:** Chapter 5 of the theoretical cornerstone of the KO field. Covers bibliographic languages, work/document/subject languages, the principle of user convenience, the principle of representation, and the ontological status of documents.
- **License:** Educational use (hosted by Evergreen State College)

### Hjørland — Theories of Knowledge Organization: Theories of Knowledge
- **File:** `hjorland-theories-of-ko.pdf`
- **Source:** https://www.inlibra.com/10.5771/0943-7444-2013-3-169.pdf
- **DOI:** 10.5771/0943-7444-2013-3-169
- **Author:** Birger Hjørland
- **Published:** Knowledge Organization, Vol. 40, No. 3, 2013
- **Description:** Hjørland's comprehensive review of KO theories: user-based/cognitive, facet-analytical, bibliometric, and domain-analytic approaches. Argues that only the domain-analytic view is committed to understanding KO in light of subject knowledge and scholarly theories.
- **License:** Open access (journal article)
