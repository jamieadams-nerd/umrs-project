## Description

The IEKO is a peer-reviewed, open-access online encyclopedia edited by Birger Hjørland and Claudio Gnoli. It covers the full intellectual territory of knowledge organization: classification theory, thesauri, ontologies, faceted classification, bibliographic control, knowledge representation, and epistemological foundations of KO.

Articles are 3,000–10,000 words each with full citations.
