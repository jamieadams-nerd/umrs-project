### ANSI/NISO Z39.19-2005 (R2010) — Guidelines for Constructing Controlled Vocabularies
- **File:** `niso-z39-19-2005r2010.pdf`
- **Source:** http://groups.niso.org/higherlogic/ws/public/download/12591/z39-19-2005r2010.pdf
- **Published by:** National Information Standards Organization (NISO)
- **Standard number:** ANSI/NISO Z39.19-2005, reaffirmed 2010
- **Description:** The professional standard for taxonomy and thesaurus design. Covers: preferred terms, entry vocabulary, hierarchical relationships (BT/NT), associative relationships (RT), scope notes, equivalence relationships, compound terms, and faceted systems. Directly applicable to CUI category taxonomy design, UMRS posture signal catalog structure, and any controlled vocabulary used in UMRS documentation.
- **UMRS relevance:** The intellectual machinery behind CUI label design and posture signal classification is the same as thesaurus design. This standard provides the formal rules.
- **License:** Free download from NISO (standards body)
