### Blackwell & Green — Cognitive Dimensions of Notations (Chapter)
- **File:** `blackwell-green-cognitive-dimensions.pdf`
- **Source:** https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~afb21/publications/BlackwellGreen-CDsChapter.pdf
- **Authors:** Alan Blackwell, Thomas Green
- **Description:** Framework for evaluating the usability of any notation system — programming languages, UIs, data formats. Directly applicable to evaluating security context strings, CUI labels, and TUI display formats.
- **License:** Author-distributed

### Vannevar Bush — As We May Think (1945)
- **File:** `vannevar-bush-as-we-may-think-1945.pdf`
- **Source:** https://worrydream.com/refs/Bush%20-%20As%20We%20May%20Think%20(Life%20Magazine%209-10-1945).pdf
- **Original publication:** The Atlantic Monthly, July 1945 (also Life Magazine version)
- **Description:** Foundational document of the modern information retrieval vision. Bush proposes the Memex — a device for linking and retrieving associated information. The conceptual ancestor of hypertext, the web, and modern search. Assigned in Stanford CS347.
- **License:** Public domain (1945)
