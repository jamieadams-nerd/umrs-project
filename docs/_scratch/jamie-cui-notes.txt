How to Construct the Banner
When marking a document, you ignore the "Index Grouping" name and use the Category Abbreviation.

   1. CUI Basic: Only the word CUI is required, though you may add the category (e.g., CUI//LEI).
   2. CUI Specified: You must include the category with an SP- prefix (e.g., CUI//SP-CTI).
   3. Multiple Categories: List them alphabetically, separated by a single slash (e.g., CUI//SP-CTI/EXPT).
