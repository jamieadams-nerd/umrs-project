= What Is High Assurance?
:description: High-assurance engineering introduction — what it means, why it matters, and what it requires.
:toc: left
:toclevels: 2

High assurance is an engineering standard, not a marketing claim. A high-assurance system provides mechanically verifiable guarantees about its security properties — guarantees that hold even under adversarial conditions. This page explains the concept and its relevance to UMRS.
