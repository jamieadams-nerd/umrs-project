= Logging and Audit
:description: Audit trail design, log capacity planning, and log lifecycle management for UMRS.

This module covers the design and operation of the UMRS audit trail — how audit events are
structured, how logs are sized and tuned, and how the log lifecycle is managed from
generation through retention and disposal.

== Topics

* xref:logging-capacity.adoc[Log capacity planning] — sizing audit storage for your deployment.

* xref:log-lifecycle-model.adoc[Log lifecycle model] — generation, retention, archival, and
  disposal policy for audit records.

* xref:log-tuning.adoc[Log filesystem and journald tuning] — kernel and journald configuration
  for reliable, high-throughput audit logging.
