== UMRS-Relevant Categories

The following categories are particularly relevant to UMRS deployments in security,
infrastructure, and IT log environments. Descriptions are paraphrased from NARA CUI
Registry guidance.

`EXPT — Export Controlled`::
Information subject to U.S. export control laws such as ITAR or EAR. This includes
technical data, software, or other information whose transfer to foreign persons or
countries is restricted.

`EXPTR — Export Controlled Research`::
Research data that falls under export control, often arising in academic or R&D
contexts where results could be export-controlled.

`CTI — Controlled Technical Information`::
Technical information (such as engineering data and software) with military or space
application, requiring protection under specific defense-related regulations or
contracts.

`ISVI — Information Systems Vulnerability Information`::
Details about weaknesses, vulnerabilities, or exploitation paths in information systems
or networks. Requires protection to avoid aiding adversaries.

`OPSEC — Operations Security`::
Information whose uncontrolled exposure would undermine operational security — for
example, patterns revealing how an organization operates, schedules, or specific
capabilities.

`PCII — Protected Critical Infrastructure Information`::
Critical infrastructure information voluntarily submitted to DHS under PCII authority,
which receives special protections and handling controls.

`CEII — Critical Energy Infrastructure Information`::
Information about energy sector infrastructure (generation, transmission, pipelines)
that could be misused to disrupt services or compromise safety.

`PHYS — Physical Security`::
Information related to physical security plans, vulnerabilities, or protective measures
for facilities or assets.

`HLTH — Health Information`::
Health-related personal information requiring protection, often overlapping with HIPAA
concepts (for example, medical records or health status).

`PRVCY — General Privacy`::
General personally identifiable information (PII) that requires safeguarding but is
not covered by a more specific privacy category.

`MIL — Military Personnel Records`::
Personnel data for military members that requires safeguarding (assignments,
performance, non-public details).

`PERS — Personnel Records`::
Personnel records for non-military staff or employees.

`STUD — Student Records`::
Educational records subject to privacy or FERPA-like protections.

`GENETIC — Genetic Information`::
Information about genetic tests or characteristics (DNA, inherited traits) that are
individually identifiable and protected.
